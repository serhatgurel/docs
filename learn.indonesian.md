# The Course Plan

Modeled after common Indonesian curricula (A1→B1 progression), focusing on foundational communicative competence and core morphology:

- **A1 Basics:** Alphabet & pronunciation, greetings & introductions, numbers, dates & time, family terms, pronouns, to be/negation, common verbs.
- **A1-A2 Core Grammar:** Sentence order (S–P–O–K), question words (apa/siapa/di mana/kapan/mengapa/bagaimana), negation (tidak/bukan), aspect (sudah/belum), modality (bisa/harus/mau), classifiers & measure words.
- **A2 Affixes I:** Active `me-` (meng-/men-/mem-/meny-/me-), `ber-`, simple `di-` passive; frequent derivations and usage frequency over rules.
- **A2 Daily Life:** Routines, requests/offers, directions & transport, shopping & prices, food & ordering, health & body.
- **B1 Affixes II:** `ke- -an`, `pe- -an`, `ter-`, causatives, noun/verb derivations; passive (`di-`) vs. active (`me-`) usage.
- **B1 Communication:** Appointments, plans, workplace basics, describing processes, simple reports & narratives; register & politeness strategies.

## Detailed Syllabus (Lessons)

Each lesson follows the structure above and must include all words used anywhere in the lesson in the final JSON vocabulary.

### Phase A1 Basics

- **Lesson 01: Sapaan & Perkenalan (Greetings & Introductions)**
  - Objectives: say hello, introduce yourself, ask names, exchange pleasantries.
- **Lesson 02: Alfabet, Pelafalan Dasar & Ejaan (Alphabet & Pronunciation)**
  - Objectives: pronounce core sounds, spell names, read common syllables.
- **Lesson 03: Angka, Tanggal & Waktu (Numbers, Dates & Time)**
  - Objectives: count, tell time, say dates, schedule simply.
- **Lesson 04: Keluarga & Hubungan (Family & Relations)**
  - Objectives: refer to family members, address relatives politely.
- **Lesson 05: Kata Ganti Orang & Kepemilikan (Pronouns & Possession)**
  - Objectives: use saya/kamu/Anda/dia/kita/kami; possessives with "punya" and "-nya".
- **Lesson 06: Kalimat Dasar (S–P–O–K) & Negasi (Basic Sentences & Negation)**
  - Objectives: build statements, use tidak/bukan correctly, add place/time.

### Phase A1–A2 Core Grammar & Daily Life

- **Lesson 07: Kata Tanya (Question Words)**
  - Objectives: apa/siapa/di mana/kapan/mengapa/bagaimana in natural questions.
- **Lesson 08: Aktivitas Sehari-hari (Daily Routines)**
  - Objectives: talk about habits with frequent verbs; adverbs of frequency.
- **Lesson 09: Permintaan, Tawaran & Izin (Requests, Offers & Permission)**
  - Objectives: use boleh/bisa/harap/tolong; softeners (maaf, permisi).
- **Lesson 10: Arah, Transportasi & Lokasi (Directions, Transport & Location)**
  - Objectives: ask/give directions; prepositions (di, ke, dari, depan, belakang, dekat).
- **Lesson 11: Belanja, Harga & Negosiasi (Shopping & Prices)**
  - Objectives: ask price, quantities, bargain politely; classifiers.
- **Lesson 12: Makanan, Minuman & Memesan (Food, Drinks & Ordering)**
  - Objectives: order items, preferences, dietary notes; common menu vocabulary.
- **Lesson 13: Kesehatan Dasar & Tubuh (Health & Body)**
  - Objectives: describe symptoms, ask for help, basic pharmacy language.

### Phase A2 Affixes & Patterns

- **Lesson 14: Awalan me- (Aktif)**
  - Objectives: recognize and use me-/meng-/men-/mem-/meny- forms in high-frequency verbs.
- **Lesson 15: Awalan ber- (Aktivitas/Kondisi)**
  - Objectives: use ber- for states/activities (berjalan, bekerja, berbicara).
- **Lesson 16: Pasif sederhana di- (Overview)**
  - Objectives: recognize di- passive in common contexts; active vs passive choice.
- **Lesson 17: Aspek & Modalitas (sudah/belum, mau/harus/bisa)**
  - Objectives: talk about completion, intention, obligation, ability naturally.

### Phase B1 Foundations

- **Lesson 18: Derivasi ke- -an & pe- -an (Nouns/States/Processes)**
  - Objectives: form common nouns/states; use high-value patterns.
- **Lesson 19: ter- & Superlatif/Keadaan**
  - Objectives: use ter- for state/result; common fixed expressions.
- **Lesson 20: Narasi Sederhana (Simple Narratives)**
  - Objectives: recount past events, sequence with kemudian/lalu/terus.
- **Lesson 21: Janji Temu & Rencana (Appointments & Plans)**
  - Objectives: set meetings, propose times, accept/decline politely.
- **Lesson 22: Komunikasi di Tempat Kerja (Workplace Basics)**
  - Objectives: simple status updates, requests, task descriptions.
- **Lesson 23: Mendeskripsikan Proses (Describing Processes)**
  - Objectives: explain steps with cohesive devices (pertama, selanjutnya, akhirnya).
- **Lesson 24: Ragam & Kesantunan (Register & Politeness)**
  - Objectives: choose polite forms; soften/strengthen statements appropriately.

### Phase B2 Expansion

- **Lesson 25: Wacana Berita & Formalitas (News Discourse & Formal Register)**
  - Objectives: parse headlines and formal constructions; summarize news.
- **Lesson 26: Pasif Kompleks & Variasi (Complex Passives & Variants)**
  - Objectives: contrast di-/ter-/ke- -an passives; control voice shifts.
- **Lesson 27: Argumen & Opini (Arguments & Opinions)**
  - Objectives: express stance, hedging, concession, cause–effect.
- **Lesson 28: Surat & Email Profesional (Professional Writing)**
  - Objectives: write clear emails, requests, updates with appropriate tone.
- **Lesson 29: Presentasi Pendek (Short Presentations)**
  - Objectives: outline, signpost, deliver 3–5 minute talks.
- **Lesson 30: Idiom & Kolokasi Umum (Idioms & Common Collocations)**
  - Objectives: recognize and use high-frequency idioms/collocations.

### Phase C1 Preparation (Toward Advanced Fluency)

- **Lesson 31: Gaya Birokrasi & Hukum (Bureaucratic Style & Legalese)**
  - Objectives: interpret formal notices; extract actionable information.
- **Lesson 32: Analisis Teks Panjang (Long-Form Text Analysis)**
  - Objectives: scan, skim, and deep-read magazines/non-fiction chapters.
- **Lesson 33: Diskusi & Negosiasi Lanjutan (Advanced Discussion & Negotiation)**
  - Objectives: manage turn-taking, clarify, reformulate, and negotiate outcomes.
- **Lesson 34: Penulisan Laporan (Report Writing)**
  - Objectives: structure multi-section reports with clear cohesion.
- **Lesson 35: Media Tanpa Teks (Audio/Video Without Subtitles)**
  - Objectives: comprehend gist/details at native speed; note-taking strategies.
- **Lesson 36: Review & Penilaian (Comprehensive Review & Assessment)**
  - Objectives: integrated skills assessment; individualized remediation plan.

### Phase C2 Mastery (Near-Native Control)

- **Lesson 37: Nuansa Idiomatik & Pragmatik (Idiomatic Nuance & Pragmatics)**
  - Objectives: select context-appropriate idioms; manage politeness and implicature.
- **Lesson 38: Ragam Tekstil & Editorial (Editorial & Opinion Writing)**
  - Objectives: craft coherent arguments; edit for tone, register, style.
- **Lesson 39: Multidomain Media Comprehension**
  - Objectives: follow complex broadcasts/podcasts without subtitles; summarize accurately.
- **Lesson 40: Debat Terstruktur (Structured Debate)**
  - Objectives: defend positions, rebut, and synthesize in real time.
- **Lesson 41: Dokumentasi Profesional Lanjutan (Advanced Professional Documentation)**
  - Objectives: produce proposals, executive summaries, and policy-style briefs.
- **Lesson 42: Portofolio C2 & Evaluasi**
  - Objectives: compile portfolio; final assessment and individualized roadmap.
